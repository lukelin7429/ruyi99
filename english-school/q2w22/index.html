Quarter 2 Week 22

Moving in Harmony

在和諧中律動 · The Body’s Grace 身體的優雅

Principle：Moving Meditation · Body as Medicine 身體即良藥 · English：“I feel harmony…” · “Everyone is unique.” · Level：Ages 5–16

本週重點

Overview

💬 English Goal

英文目標

Learn the words harmony and unique, and correct the spelling of wonderful and excellent. Describe inner states with “I feel harmony in my heart” and “Everyone is unique.”

學習 harmony（和諧）和 unique（獨一無二的）這兩個字，並改正 wonderful（很棒的）和 excellent（優秀的）的拼字。用「I feel harmony in my heart（我感覺到心裡的和諧）」和「Everyone is unique.（每個人都是獨一無二的。）」來描述內在的狀態。

🪷 Spiritual Goal

佛學／心靈目標

Understand harmony as body and mind in agreement, and deepen the idea of the body as medicine. Through Anattā (無我), honor that each being is unique, yet all can live together in peace.

了解「和諧」就是身與心的一致，並深入理解「身體即良藥」的觀念。透過「無我（Anattā）」，珍視每一個生命都是獨一無二的，但所有生命都能和平地共處。

單字 Vocabulary

點 🔊 聽發音

句型 Sentence Patterns

Key Patterns
Pattern 1 · Feeling harmony inside
I feel harmony in ___.

I feel harmony in my heart. 我感覺到心裡的和諧。

I feel calm when I move slowly. 當我慢慢動的時候，我覺得很平靜。

I move in harmony with the class. 我和全班一起和諧地律動。

Pattern 2 · Everyone is special
___ is unique.

Everyone is unique. 每個人都是獨一無二的。

My painting is unique because I used my favorite colors. 我的畫是獨一無二的，因為我用了我最喜歡的顏色。

Pattern 3 · Living together (noun usage)
We live in harmony.

Our class sings in harmony together. 我們全班一起和諧地歌唱。

Unique people can live in harmony. 獨一無二的人們可以和諧地生活在一起。

✏️ Grammar Focus · Adjective vs. noun

Unique is an adjective — it describes a person or thing: Everyone is unique. Harmony is a noun — a thing we have or live in: We live in harmony. Watch your spelling, too: it is w-o-n-d-e-r-f-u-l and e-x-c-e-l-l-e-n-t.

Unique（獨一無二的）是形容詞——它用來描述一個人或一樣東西：Everyone is unique.（每個人都是獨一無二的。）Harmony（和諧）是名詞——是我們擁有或身處其中的一樣東西：We live in harmony.（我們和諧地生活。）也要小心你的拼字：是 w-o-n-d-e-r-f-u-l，以及 e-x-c-e-l-l-e-n-t。

故事 The Story

Story

One day the teacher asked the students to sit as silent as a mountain. First they listened to their own breathing. Then the teacher began to speak softly — a guided meditation — and the children let the calm voice lead their minds back to the present moment.

有一天，老師請學生們像山一樣安靜（silent）地坐著。他們先是聆聽自己的呼吸。接著老師開始輕聲說話——一段引導式（guided）的靜坐——孩子們讓這平靜的聲音把他們的心帶回到當下。

Next, the teacher stood up and led a slow Tai Chi flow. Arms lifted like rising water; feet stepped like floating clouds. Every child moved differently, yet the whole class flowed together — this is harmony. The teacher smiled and said, “When your body and your breath agree, you have found harmony in motion.”

接下來，老師站起來，帶著大家做一套緩慢的太極流動。手臂像上升的流水一樣抬起，腳步像飄浮的雲一樣移動。每個孩子的動作都不一樣，可是全班卻一起流動了起來——這就是和諧（harmony）。老師微笑著說：「當你的身體和你的呼吸一致時，你就在律動中找到了和諧。」

The teacher reminded them: each child is unique — no one else in the world moves, breathes, or thinks in exactly the same way. And still, all these unique bodies can move as one. That is the gift of the body as medicine: a healthy body and a calm breath become the home of a peaceful heart.

老師提醒他們：每個孩子都是獨一無二的（unique）——世界上沒有別人會用完全一樣的方式律動、呼吸或思考。但即使如此，這些獨一無二的身體仍然能像一體一樣律動。這就是「身體即良藥」所帶來的禮物：一個健康的身體和一份平靜的呼吸，會成為一顆平安的心的家。

🤔 Check your understanding

  • What does “harmony in motion” mean? 「律動中的和諧」是什麼意思？
  • How did the guided meditation help the students? 老師的引導冥想如何幫助學生？
  • Why is it good that everyone is unique? 每個人都獨一無二，為什麼是好事？

佛法小品 The Principle

Principle

🌟 Body, Breath, and Group as One 身心一致

Harmony is not everyone being the same. It is many unique people — each with their own佛性 (Buddha-nature) — choosing to move, breathe, and live together gently. When your body is well and your breath is calm, the mind rests easily. Move your body with kindness, and the harmony you feel inside will spread to your family and your school.

和諧並不是每個人都一模一樣。它是許多獨一無二的人——每個人都有自己的佛性（Buddha-nature）——選擇溫柔地一起律動、呼吸與生活。當你的身體安好、你的呼吸平靜時，心就能輕鬆地安歇。帶著善意律動你的身體，你內在感受到的和諧就會擴散到你的家人和你的學校。

練習 Practice

Homework

Write your sentences this week, and practice 3 minutes of silent meditation at home — sit as still as a mountain. Choose the tier that fits you. 選一個適合自己的等級。

Beginner 入門

Draw & trace

Ages 5–8 · new learners
Draw a picture of yourself and write “I am unique.” Then trace and complete two sentences about harmony.
畫一張你自己的圖，並寫上「I am unique.（我是獨一無二的。）」然後描寫並完成兩個關於和諧的句子。
ExampleI want harmony in my world. Everyone is unique.
Intermediate 中級

Spelling & reflection

Ages 9–12 · developing fluency
Write wonderful, excellent, unique, harmony three times each. Then write 3 sentences about the guided meditation using the word silent.
把 wonderful（很棒的）、excellent（優秀的）、unique（獨一無二的）、harmony（和諧）各寫三次。然後用 silent（安靜的）這個字，寫 3 個關於引導式靜坐的句子。
ExampleI sat silent and calm. The teacher’s voice was wonderful. I felt harmony in my heart.
Advanced 進階

Integration paragraph

Ages 13–16 · fluent speakers
Write a 6–10 sentence paragraph using all four words. Explain why unique people should live in harmony, using the Tai Chi class as your example.
寫一段 6–10 個句子的短文，用上這四個字。以太極課為例，解釋為什麼獨一無二的人們應該和諧地生活在一起。
ExampleAlthough we are each unique, our class moved as one in the Tai Chi flow. That excellent, wonderful feeling was harmony in motion.

小考 Mini Quiz

Check Yourself

選出最適合的答案，點選後會看到解析。

Question 1 of 10
Which word means “和諧 / things working well together”?
Correct: B. Harmony means things working well together (和諧). Unique = one of a kind; silent = quiet.
Question 2 of 10
Which word means “獨一無二 / one of a kind”?
Correct: A. Unique (獨一無二的) describes something special and one of a kind, like you.
Question 3 of 10
Choose the correct sentence.
Correct: C. Unique is an adjective: “Everyone is unique.” We do not say “is harmony” about a person.
Question 4 of 10
Complete the sentence: “We live in ___.”
Correct: D. Harmony is a noun: “We live in harmony.” The other words are adjectives.
Question 5 of 10
Which spelling is correct?
Correct: B. It is spelled w-o-n-d-e-r-f-u-l — one “l” at the end.
Question 6 of 10
Which spelling is correct?
Correct: A. Excellent has two “l”s and ends in “-ent”: e-x-c-e-l-l-e-n-t.
Question 7 of 10
In a guided meditation, the students…
Correct: C. Guided (引導的) means the teacher’s voice leads the students’ minds back to the present moment.
Question 8 of 10
“Harmony in motion” in the Tai Chi class means…
Correct: D. Each child is unique, yet the whole class flows together — body, breath, and group acting as one.
Question 9 of 10
Add a reason: “My painting is unique ___ I used my favorite colors.”
Correct: C. Because introduces a reason: “…unique because I used my favorite colors.”
Question 10 of 10
“The body as medicine” teaches us that…
Correct: B. When the body is well and the breath is calm, the mind rests easily — a healthy body is the home of a peaceful heart.
