Quarter 1 Week 8

Sustained Effort and the Present Moment

持續精進與當下 · Viriya & Sati 正精進與正念

Principle：Right Effort · Viriya 正精進 · Mindfulness · Sati 正念 · English：Adverbs of manner · the four main tenses · Level：Ages 5–16

本週重點

Overview

💬 English Goal

英文目標

Form and use adverbs of manner by adding -ly (wise → wisely), including irregular ones (good → well, fast → fast). Review the four main tenses — simple present, present continuous, simple past, simple future — to describe when an action happens.

透過加上 -ly 來構成並使用情狀副詞（adverbs of manner）（wise → wisely 有智慧地），也包括不規則的（good → well 好地、fast → fast 快速地）。複習四個主要時態──簡單現在式、現在進行式、簡單過去式、簡單未來式──用來描述一個動作發生的時間。

🪷 Spiritual Goal

佛學／心靈目標

Understand Right Effort (Viriya) — preventing unwholesome states and cultivating wholesome ones — and define Mindfulness (Sati) as awareness of the present moment. Use the wisdom learned earlier to guide our effort, and act it out steadily, every day.

了解「正精進」（Right Effort，Viriya）──防止不善的狀態、培養善的狀態──並把「正念」（Mindfulness，Sati）定義為對當下的覺察。運用先前學到的智慧來引導我們的精進，並且每天穩定地把它實踐出來。

句型 Sentence Patterns

Key Patterns
Pattern 1 · Saying how you act (adverb of manner)
I + verb + ___ly.

We speak truthfully. We speak truthfully.（我們誠實地說話。）

I always listen carefully. I always listen carefully.（我總是仔細地聆聽。）

He acted wisely and kindly. He acted wisely and kindly.（他有智慧又善良地行動。）

Pattern 2 · Being present (present continuous)
I am ___ing ___ly now.

I am sitting quietly now. I am sitting quietly now.（我現在正安靜地坐著。）

I am breathing slowly at this moment. I am breathing slowly at this moment.（我此刻正緩緩地呼吸。）

Pattern 3 · Promising future effort (simple future)
I will ___ ___ly next time.

I will speak kindly next time. I will speak kindly next time.（下次我會親切地說話。）

Yesterday, I responded thoughtfully instead of shouting. Yesterday, I responded thoughtfully instead of shouting.（昨天我沒有大吼，而是體貼地回應。）

✏️ Grammar Focus · Adverbs of manner & the four tenses

An adverb of manner tells us how we do something. Most are made with adjective + -ly: wise → wisely · calm → calmly · truthful → truthfully. Watch the irregular ones: good → well and fast → fast. Then choose the right tense to show when: simple present for habits (always, every day), present continuous for now (at the moment), simple past for reflection (yesterday), and simple future for plans (tomorrow, next week).

情狀副詞（adverb of manner）告訴我們做一件事的方式（how）。大部分是用「形容詞 + -ly」構成：wise → wisely（有智慧地）、calm → calmly（平靜地）、truthful → truthfully（誠實地）。要注意不規則的：good → well（好地）和 fast → fast（快速地）。接著選對時態（tense）來表示時間：用簡單現在式表示習慣（always 總是、every day 每天），用現在進行式表示現在（at the moment 此刻），用簡單過去式表示回顧（yesterday 昨天），用簡單未來式表示計畫（tomorrow 明天、next week 下週）。

故事 The Story

Story

Imagine a young student named Anya. Last week she learned to be wise. This week her teacher asks a new question: not only what is wise, but how do you act? Anya thinks for a moment and says, “I will act wisely.” The teacher smiles — Anya has turned an adjective into an adverb of manner, a word that shows how we do things.

想像一位名叫安雅（Anya）的小學生。上週她學會了要有智慧。這週老師問了一個新問題：不只是什麼才是有智慧的，而是你要怎麼去做？安雅想了一下，說：「我會有智慧地（wisely）行動。」老師笑了──安雅把一個形容詞變成了情狀副詞（adverb of manner），一個用來表示我們怎麼做事的字。

One day Anya felt angry and wanted to shout. But she remembered Right Effort (Viriya): stop the unkind thing before it grows, and grow the kind thing instead （阻止惡、生起善）. So she breathed calmly and answered gently. “Last week I shouted,” she said. “Today I am speaking kindly, and tomorrow I will try again.” One sentence held the past, the present, and the future — and a steady, joyful effort.

有一天安雅覺得很生氣，想要大吼。但她想起了「正精進」（Right Effort，Viriya）：在不善的東西長大之前先把它停下來，並且改去培養善的東西（阻止惡、生起善）。於是她平靜地（calmly）呼吸，溫和地（gently）回答。「上週我大吼了，」她說，「今天我正親切地（kindly）說話，明天我會再試一次。」一個句子同時包含了過去、現在和未來──還有一份穩定而喜悅的精進。

Then her teacher asked her to sit quietly. Anya noticed her breath, the sounds in the room, the warm light. She was not thinking about yesterday or tomorrow. She was simply here. That is Mindfulness (Sati) （正念）: full, gentle awareness of the present moment. Effort and mindfulness work as one — we cultivate good habits steadily, and we abandon harmful ones, one mindful moment at a time.

接著老師請她安靜地坐著。安雅注意到自己的呼吸、房間裡的聲音、溫暖的光。她沒有在想昨天或明天。她就只是在這裡（here）。這就是「正念」（Mindfulness，Sati）：對當下完整而溫柔的覺察。精進和正念合而為一──我們穩定地培養（cultivate）好習慣，並捨棄（abandon）有害的習慣，一次一個正念的當下。

佛法小品 The Principle

Principle

Right Effort is not a single burst — it is steady, conscious energy, repeated every day. Mindfulness is the gentle light that keeps that effort awake in the present moment.

正精進不是一次爆發──它是穩定、有意識的能量，每天重複。正念則是那道溫柔的光，讓那份精進在當下保持清醒。

🌟 Steady Effort, Awake Mind 持續的精進，清醒的心

Right Effort (Viriya) has four sides: prevent unwholesome states from arising, abandon those that have arisen, cultivate wholesome states, and keep them growing. Notice that we describe this with adverbs — we act steadily, wisely, calmly — and we anchor it with tenses: a habit in the simple present, a mindful action in the present continuous. When we act wisely every day and stay aware right now, effort and mindfulness become one path that leads toward Concentration (Samādhi).

正精進（Viriya）有四個面向：防止還沒生起的不善狀態生起、捨棄已經生起的不善狀態、培養善的狀態、並讓它們持續增長。注意我們是用副詞（adverbs）來描述這件事──我們穩定地、有智慧地、平靜地行動──並用時態（tenses）來定位它：習慣用簡單現在式，正念的行動用現在進行式。當我們每天都有智慧地行動，並在此刻保持覺察，精進和正念就成為同一條路，通往「定」（Concentration，Samādhi）。

練習 Practice

Homework

Each day this week, record one act of Right Effort. Use an adverb to say how you acted, and a tense to show when. Choose the tier that fits you — the daily practice is the effort. 每天記錄一個正精進的行為，用副詞描述方式，用時態確認時間。

Beginner 入門

Present + reflection

Ages 5–8 · new learners
Write 2 sentences in the simple present, each with an adverb, about a kind daily habit. Then write 1 sentence in the simple past about a calm moment today.
用簡單現在式寫 2 個句子，每句都帶一個副詞，描述一個善良的日常習慣。然後用簡單過去式寫 1 個句子，描述今天一個平靜的時刻。
ExampleI always listen carefully. I speak kindly to my friend. I felt calm when I read quietly in my room.
Intermediate 中級

Now + past effort

Ages 9–12 · developing fluency
Write 1 sentence in the present continuous with an adverb about your focus right now. Then write 2 sentences in the simple past with adverbs about a Right Effort that worked.
用現在進行式寫 1 個句子，帶一個副詞，描述你現在正在專注的事。然後用簡單過去式寫 2 個句子，帶副詞，描述一次成功的正精進。
ExampleI am sitting peacefully right now, focusing steadily on my breath. Yesterday, I responded thoughtfully instead of shouting. I finished my homework diligently.
Advanced 進階

Synthesis

Ages 13–16 · fluent speakers
Write 4–5 sentences combining future, past, and present continuous with several adverbs to discuss the four sides of Right Effort. Then reflect: how do effort and mindfulness help you overcome greed (貪) or anger (嗔)?
寫 4 到 5 個句子，結合未來式、過去式和現在進行式，並用上好幾個副詞，來談正精進的四個面向。然後反思：精進和正念如何幫助你克服貪（greed）或嗔（anger）？
ExampleI will try harder to speak gently and truthfully tomorrow. I am working patiently on my difficult assignment now, preventing frustration. Last week, I successfully avoided a mean comment, which I now see as acting wisely.

小考 Mini Quiz

Check Yourself

選出最適合的答案，點選後會看到解析。

Question 1 of 10
Make the adjective wise into an adverb of manner.
Correct: C. Most adverbs of manner are made with adjective + -ly: wise → wisely (明智地).
Question 2 of 10
An adverb of manner tells us…
Correct: A. An adverb of manner describes how an action is done — for example, “She acted kindly.”
Question 3 of 10
Which is the correct irregular adverb for good ?
Correct: D. Good is an adjective; its adverb of manner is the irregular form well. (We also keep fast → fast.)
Question 4 of 10
For a habit we do every day , which tense do we use?
Correct: B. The simple present describes habits — the steady Right Effort we repeat every day.
Question 5 of 10
To describe mindfulness right now , choose: “I ___ quietly at the moment.”
Correct: C. The present continuous (am/is/are + -ing) shows what is happening now — perfect for Mindfulness (Sati).
Question 6 of 10
Promise future effort: “I ___ kindly next time.”
Correct: A. The simple future (will + verb) is for plans: “I will speak kindly next time.”
Question 7 of 10
What does Right Effort (Viriya) mean?
Correct: D. Right Effort prevents and abandons unwholesome states, and cultivates wholesome ones — steady, conscious energy.
Question 8 of 10
Mindfulness (Sati) is best described as…
Correct: B. Mindfulness is gentle, full awareness of the present moment (當下) — being here, now.
Question 9 of 10
Which word means 培養 / to grow a good habit ?
Correct: C. Cultivate (培養) means to grow or develop. Its opposite here is abandon (捨棄), to let go.
Question 10 of 10
Choose the sentence that joins a good adverb with a correct tense for a habit.
Correct: A. “I always act wisely” uses the simple present for a habit plus the adverb of manner wisely — Right Effort, every day.
