Quarter 2 Week 14

Kindness and Interconnectedness

善良與同體大悲 · Love is Beautiful: Why We Care for All Beings 愛是美好的：為什麼我們要關愛眾生

Principle：Interconnectedness · Pratītyasamutpāda 緣起 · English：“We should…” · “May all beings be free from…” · Level：Ages 5–16

本週重點

Overview

💬 English Goal

英文目標

Use “should” to talk about what is right: “We should love everyone because we are all human.” Learn words for caring and for suffering: kind, mean, connected, suffering, ill-will. Practice the well-wish pattern “May all beings be free from…”

🪷 Spiritual Goal

佛學／心靈目標

Understand interconnectedness (緣起 · Pratītyasamutpāda): helping others helps yourself. Extend loving-kindness beyond friends to strangers and animals — a first taste of 同體大悲, the equality of all beings.

單字 Vocabulary

句型 Sentence Patterns

Key Patterns
Pattern 1 · Saying what is right (should)
We should ___ because ___.

We should love everyone because we are all human.

We should be kind because everyone wants to be happy.

We should not hurt others, because we are all connected.

Pattern 2 · Sending well-wishes
May all beings be free from ___.

May all beings be free from anger.

May all beings be free from jealousy.

May you be free from mental and physical suffering.

Pattern 3 · Saying no to harm (negative imperatives)
No ___ing. / We should not ___.

No hitting, no screaming, no hurting others.

We should not hurt animals.

✏️ Grammar Focus · The helping verb “should”

We use should to talk about the right thing to do. After should, always use the plain (base) verb: We should help. · We should be kind. For the negative, add not: We should not hurt others. Notice that should never adds -s, even for he or she: She should be kind.

故事 The Story

Story

Imagine a great web （一張大網） stretched across the whole world. At every knot of the web there is a person, an animal, a tree. The threads connect them all. When one thread is pulled, every other thread feels the gentle tug. Nothing in the web is truly alone.

One day a child was unkind to another child — she said mean words and made him cry. But the web was connected, so the unkindness traveled along the threads. The first child felt heavy and unhappy too, because hurting others is hurting ourselves （傷害他人就是傷害自己）.

The next day she chose differently. She helped her friend, shared her food, and was gentle with the class cat. This time kindness traveled along the threads, and warmth came back to her own heart. We all eat, sleep, and wish to be happy — we are made of the same needs. When we care for any being, we care for the whole web, and for ourselves. That is the meaning of interconnectedness.

  • How can you send kindness along the web this week? 這週你可以怎麼把善意傳遞出去？

佛法小品 The Principle

Principle

All beings share the same needs and the same wish to be free from suffering. Because we are connected, kindness is not only good for others — it is the natural way to care for ourselves and the whole world.

🌟 One Body, Great Compassion 同體大悲

In Buddhism, Pratītyasamutpāda (緣起) teaches that everything arises together — nothing stands apart. When we understand this, compassion is not a chore but common sense: a person who hurts and screams brings ill-will back into their own heart, while a person who shares kindness lives in peace. To bow with hands together (合十) is to say, “I see the goodness in you, and we belong to one another.” Loving strangers and animals is the first step of 同體大悲 — the great compassion that holds all beings as one body.

練習 Practice

Homework

Show your love this week, then write about it. Choose the tier that fits you — kindness in action is the lesson. 這週實際付出善意，再寫下來。選一個適合自己的等級。

Beginner 入門

Words & one act

Ages 5–8 · new learners
Fill in the blanks, then do one kind act. Use the words Human, Animals, Peace, Connected.
ExampleWe should not hurt Animals. I wish for everyone to live in Peace. I helped my mom today.
Intermediate 中級

“Love is Beautiful” paragraph

Ages 9–12 · developing fluency
Write two short paragraphs: (1) Why is love important? (2) How will you show love this week? Use should and because.
ExampleLove is important because it makes the world happy. We should be kind because we are all connected. I will show love by being kind to my cat.
Advanced 進階

Essay & well-wishes

Ages 13–16 · fluent speakers
Write a full “Love is Beautiful” essay (5+ sentences) and add four well-wishes using “May all beings be free from…” Reflect on interconnectedness.
ExampleBecause we are all connected, my kindness returns to me. May all beings be free from anger, jealousy, and mental and physical suffering.

小考 Mini Quiz

Check Yourself

選出最適合的答案，點選後會看到解析。

Question 1 of 10
Which word means “刻薄的 / unkind and hurtful”?
Correct: B. Mean describes someone who is unkind or hurtful (刻薄的). Kindness is the opposite.
Question 2 of 10
Complete the sentence: “We ___ love everyone because we are all human.”
Correct: D. Use should + base verb: “We should love everyone.” Should never adds -s and never takes to.
Question 3 of 10
Which sentence sends a kind well-wish correctly?
Correct: A. The pattern is “May all beings be free from ___ (noun).” Use be, from, and the noun anger.
Question 4 of 10
In our story, the great web stands for…
Correct: C. The web shows interconnectedness — pull one thread and every other thread feels it. We are all connected.
Question 5 of 10
Why does the lesson say hurting others also hurts us?
Correct: B. Interconnectedness (緣起) means unkindness travels along the web and returns to our own heart.
Question 6 of 10
Which word means “嫉妒”?
Correct: D. Jealousy = 嫉妒. Suffering = 痛苦; peace = 和平.
Question 7 of 10
Choose the correct way to say no to harm.
Correct: A. After no we use the -ing form: “No hitting, no screaming, no hurting others.”
Question 8 of 10
Why are all people “connected,” according to the lesson?
Correct: C. We share the same basic needs (food, sleep) and the same wish to be happy — so we are connected as one body.
Question 9 of 10
What does bowing with hands together (合十) mean?
Correct: D. Añjali Mudrā (合十) is a gesture of peace and respect — recognizing the goodness in every heart.
Question 10 of 10
What is the main lesson of “Love is Beautiful”?
Correct: A. Because we are all connected, extending kindness to every being — including strangers and animals — brings peace back to our own heart.
