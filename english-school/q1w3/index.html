Quarter 1 Week 3

Past Actions, Present Results

業與果報 · Karma / Cetanā 意圖的力量

Principle：Karma & Intention · Cetanā 業與意圖 · English：Simple Past · slept · ate · went · Level：Ages 5–16

本週重點

Overview

💬 English Goal

英文目標

Use the simple past tense to talk about yesterday, and master three key irregular verbs: sleep → slept, eat → ate, go → went. Learn the words intentional, result, cause.

用簡單過去式（simple past tense）來談昨天發生的事，並學會三個重要的不規則動詞：sleep → slept（睡覺）、eat → ate（吃）、go → went（去）。學習這些單字：intentional（有意圖的）、result（結果）、cause（原因）。

🪷 Spiritual Goal

佛學／心靈目標

Understand karma / Cetanā — that every action begins with an intention. Learn to look back on the past with wisdom, not worry, so that we can understand our intentions and grow from them.

了解「業／思」（karma / Cetanā）──每一個行為都從一個意圖開始。學會用智慧而不是擔憂來回顧過去，這樣我們才能明白自己的意圖，並從中成長。

句型 Sentence Patterns

Key Patterns
Pattern 1 · Saying what you did (irregular verbs)
I ___ (slept / ate / went) ___.

I slept at nine o’clock. I slept at nine o'clock.（我九點睡覺。）

I ate an apple this morning. I ate an apple this morning.（我今天早上吃了一顆蘋果。）

I went to school with my friend. I went to school with my friend.（我和朋友一起去上學。）

Pattern 2 · Asking about yesterday
What did you ___ yesterday?

What did you eat yesterday? What did you eat yesterday?（你昨天吃了什麼？）

Did you feel calm yesterday? Did you feel calm yesterday?（你昨天覺得平靜嗎？）

Pattern 3 · Giving a reason (cause and result)
I ___ because ___.

I ate rice because I was hungry. I ate rice because I was hungry.（我吃了飯，因為我餓了。）

I slept well because I felt peaceful. I slept well because I felt peaceful.（我睡得很好，因為我覺得很安詳。）

✏️ Grammar Focus · Simple past tense and irregular verbs

The simple past tense describes a finished action — something that already happened and cannot be changed. Many verbs add -ed (help → helped), but our three key verbs are irregular: sleep → slept · eat → ate · go → went. To ask a question, use did + the base verb: Did you go to school? (not Did you went).

簡單過去式（simple past tense）描述一件已經完成的動作──某件已經發生、無法再改變的事。很多動詞會加上 -ed（help → helped），但我們這三個重要動詞是不規則的（irregular）：sleep → slept、eat → ate、go → went。要提問時，用 did 加上動詞原形：Did you go to school?（你去上學了嗎？）（不是 Did you went）。

故事 The Story

Story

Imagine a gardener who plants a seed. （想像一位園丁種下一顆種子。） If she plants a kind seed — a helpful word, a generous act — a kind plant grows. If she plants a careless seed, weeds grow instead. The seed is the cause （原因）; the plant is the result （結果）. We are all gardeners of our own day.

想像一位園丁種下一顆種子。如果她種下一顆善良的種子──一句有幫助的話、一個慷慨的舉動──就會長出善良的植物。如果她種下一顆隨便、不用心的種子，長出來的就會是雜草。種子是原因（cause）；植物是結果（result）。我們每個人都是自己這一天的園丁。

So when we look back at yesterday, we are not looking for things to worry about. We look back with wisdom: “Why did I do that? What was my intention?” Maybe yesterday I forgot my homework, and this caused stress this morning. That is a small result of a small cause — and now I can plant a wiser seed today. Don’t worry about yesterday; learn from it.

所以當我們回顧昨天時，並不是在找讓自己擔憂的事。我們是用智慧來回顧：「我為什麼那樣做？我的意圖是什麼？」也許昨天我忘了帶作業，結果今天早上很緊張。這是一個小原因帶來的小結果──而現在，今天我可以種下一顆更有智慧的種子。不要為昨天擔憂，要從昨天學習。

🤔 Check your understanding

  • What does the seed stand for in the story? 故事裡的「種子」代表什麼？
  • Is karma the same as fate? Why or why not? 業力等於命運嗎？為什麼？
  • When we look back at yesterday, should we worry — or learn? 回顧昨天時，我們該擔心還是學習？

佛法小品 The Principle

Principle

In Buddhism, karma (業) is not fate or punishment. The Buddha taught: “It is intention (Cetanā 思) that I call karma.” Every action — what we say, do, and think — begins with an intention, and that intention shapes the results we meet today.

在佛教裡，業（karma，業）不是命運，也不是懲罰。佛陀教導我們：「我說，意圖（思，Cetanā）就是業。」每一個行為──我們所說、所做、所想的──都從一個意圖開始，而那個意圖塑造了我們今天所遇到的結果。

🌟 Intention is the seed 意圖即種子

Cetanā (思) means volition or intention — the small spark inside us right before we act. We cannot change yesterday’s actions, but we can understand the intentions behind them. When we plant wholesome intentions today, we shape a kinder result tomorrow. Looking back becomes a teacher, not a burden.

思（Cetanā）的意思是意志或意圖──就在我們行動之前，內心那一點小小的火花。我們無法改變昨天的行為，但我們可以明白行為背後的意圖。當我們今天種下善的意圖，就為明天塑造出更善良的結果。回顧過去因此成為一位老師，而不是一個負擔。

練習 Practice

Homework

Each day, write down what you did using the simple past tense, and reflect on the intention behind it. Choose the tier that fits you. 每天用過去式記錄，並反思行為的意圖與結果。

Beginner 入門

3 sentences

Ages 5–8 · new learners
Write 3 complete sentences that use all three target verbs: slept, ate, went. Then say one good thing that happened today.
寫出 3 個完整的句子，用上這三個目標動詞：slept、ate、went。然後說一件今天發生的好事。
ExampleI slept at nine o’clock. I ate an apple. I went to school.
Intermediate 中級

4–5 sentences

Ages 9–12 · developing fluency
Write 4–5 sentences using at least 5 past-tense verbs. Describe one wholesome action and explain why it was helpful.
寫 4 到 5 個句子，至少用上 5 個過去式動詞。描述一個善的（wholesome）行為，並解釋它為什麼有幫助。
ExampleYesterday, I helped my dad. I felt happy because I made a good action. I slept very well after that.
Advanced 進階

5+ sentences

Ages 13–16 · fluent speakers
Write 5+ sentences using complex structures (because of, although, after that) and analyze the intention (Cetanā) behind one action.
寫 5 個以上的句子，運用較複雜的結構（because of 因為、although 雖然、after that 在那之後），並分析一個行為背後的意圖（思，Cetanā）。
ExampleI forgot to finish my homework last night. Although this caused stress this morning, I reflected on my intention and promised to be more mindful of my time today.

Check Yourself

Question 1 of 10
What is the simple past tense of sleep ?
Correct: C. Sleep is irregular: sleep → slept. There is no “sleeped.”
Question 2 of 10
Choose the correct sentence about yesterday.
Correct: A. Eat is irregular: eat → ate. We use the past tense for a finished action.
Question 3 of 10
Complete it: “Yesterday I ___ to school.”
Correct: D. Go is irregular: go → went. “Goed” is never correct.
Question 4 of 10
Which question is correct?
Correct: B. After did, use the base verb: “Did you go…?” — not “did you went.”
Question 5 of 10
In Buddhism, what does karma (Cetanā) really mean?
Correct: C. The Buddha said, “It is intention (Cetanā) that I call karma.” Karma begins with our intention, not with fate.
Question 6 of 10
In the garden story, the seed is the cause. What is the plant?
Correct: A. The seed is the cause (原因) and the plant that grows is the result (結果).
Question 7 of 10
Why do we look back at yesterday?
Correct: D. We look back with wisdom, not worry — to understand our intentions and plant a wiser seed today.
Question 8 of 10
Which word means “結果 / what happens after a cause”?
Correct: B. Result = 結果. Cause = 原因; intention = 意圖.
Question 9 of 10
Add a reason: “I slept well ___ I felt peaceful.”
Correct: C. Because introduces a reason — a cause: “I slept well because I felt peaceful.”
Question 10 of 10
A student forgot her homework and felt stressed. What is the wise response?
Correct: A. We cannot change the past, but we can understand the intention behind it and plant a wiser seed today.
