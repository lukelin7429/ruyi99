The Middle Path and Right Action

中道與正業 · Magga · The Path 八正道之道諦

Principle：The Middle Path · Right Action 正業 · English：Comparatives & Superlatives · “better / the best” · Level：Ages 5–16

本週重點

Overview

💬 English Goal

英文目標

Use comparatives (more difficult, better than) and superlatives (the most important, the best) to compare and rank actions and values. Review the four main tenses through reflection.

使用比較級 comparatives（more difficult 更難、better than 比……好）和最高級 superlatives（the most important 最重要的、the best 最好的）來比較和排序行為與價值觀。透過反思複習四種主要時態。

🪷 Spiritual Goal

佛學／心靈目標

Understand Right Action (正業) and Right Livelihood (正命), and use the Middle Path (中道) to evaluate behavior — choosing what is wholesome over what is unwholesome in daily life.

理解正業（Right Action）和正命（Right Livelihood），並用中道（Middle Path）來評估行為——在日常生活中選擇善的、捨棄不善的。

句型 Sentence Patterns

Key Patterns
Pattern 1 · Comparing two things
A is ___-er / more ___ than B.

Kindness is more important than money. Kindness is more important than money.（善良比金錢更重要。）

Being honest is better than lying. Being honest is better than lying.（誠實比說謊更好。）

Helping others is harder than ignoring them. Helping others is harder than ignoring them.（幫助別人比不理會別人更難。）

Pattern 2 · Naming the best
A is the ___-est / the most ___.

The Middle Path is the best choice. The Middle Path is the best choice.（中道是最好的選擇。）

Helping others is the most rewarding action I can take. Helping others is the most rewarding action I can take.（幫助別人是我能做的最有意義的事。）

Pattern 3 · Explaining a wise choice
I chose ___ because it is the most ___.

I chose the best action because it avoided the extremes. I chose the best action because it avoided the extremes.（我選了最好的做法，因為它避開了兩個極端。）

Being a teacher is the most wholesome job because it helps people grow. Being a teacher is the most wholesome job because it helps people grow.（當老師是最善的工作，因為它幫助人成長。）

✏️ Grammar Focus · Comparatives & Superlatives

For short words, add -er to compare and -est for the best/most: kind → kinder → the kindest. For long words, use more and the most: important → more important → the most important. Some are irregular: good → better → the best · bad → worse → the worst. Use than with comparatives: better than lying.

短的字加 -er 表示比較、加 -est 表示最（most）：kind → kinder → the kindest（善良 → 更善良 → 最善良）。長的字用 more 和 the most：important → more important → the most important（重要 → 更重要 → 最重要）。有些是不規則變化：good → better → the best（好 → 更好 → 最好）、bad → worse → the worst（壞 → 更壞 → 最壞）。比較時要用 than（比）：better than lying（比說謊更好）。

故事 The Story

Story

Before he was a teacher, the Buddha tried the path of extreme effort （苦行）. For years he ate almost nothing and pushed his body to the edge. He grew so thin and weak that he could barely think — yet he was no closer to peace.

在成為老師之前，佛陀曾經嘗試極端苦行（extreme effort）的道路。好幾年裡他幾乎不吃東西，把身體逼到極限。他變得又瘦又虛弱，連思考都很困難——但他離平靜卻一點也沒有更近。

One day he heard a music teacher on a boat speaking to a student about a lute （一種弦琴）. “If the string is too tight, it will snap. If the string is too loose, it will not play. Tune it just right, and it makes beautiful music.”

有一天，他聽到一位音樂老師在船上對學生說起一把琴（lute，一種弦琴）。「如果弦繃得太緊（too tight），它會斷掉。如果弦太鬆（too loose），它彈不出聲音。把它調得剛剛好（just right），它就能奏出美妙的音樂。」

The Buddha understood at once. A life of harsh extremes is like a broken string; a lazy life is like a string too loose to sing. The wisest way is the Middle Path — not too tight, not too loose. From that balance grows Right Action: choosing what is wholesome and avoiding what causes harm. The best choice is rarely the most extreme one; it is the most balanced one.

佛陀立刻明白了。嚴苛的極端生活就像斷掉的弦；懶散的生活就像太鬆而唱不出聲的弦。最有智慧的方式就是中道（Middle Path）——不太緊，也不太鬆。從這份平衡中長出正業（Right Action）：選擇善的、避開會造成傷害的。最好的選擇很少是最極端的那個，而是最平衡（balanced）的那個。

🤔 Check your understanding

  • What happens to a string that is too tight or too loose? 弦太緊或太鬆會怎樣？
  • What does “tuning the string just right” mean for our actions? 「調到剛剛好」對我們的行為是什麼意思？
  • Why is the most balanced choice usually the best choice? 為什麼最平衡的選擇通常是最好的選擇？

佛法小品 The Principle

Principle

The Path (Magga) is the fourth Noble Truth — the way that leads out of suffering. Two of its steps are Right Action and Right Livelihood, and both are guided by the Middle Path.

道（Magga）是第四聖諦——那條帶領我們走出苦的路。其中兩個步驟是正業（Right Action）和正命（Right Livelihood），兩者都由中道（Middle Path）來引導。

🌟 Choosing the Wholesome 抉擇善業

Right Action (正業) means not harming, not stealing, and acting with honesty and care. Right Livelihood (正命) means earning a living in a way that does not hurt other beings. When you face a choice, ask: Which action is the most wholesome? Which one keeps the balance of the Middle Path? The worst way to make money is one that harms others; the easiest way to be kind is to start with a single honest, helpful act today. Comparing actions wisely — better, best, most balanced — is how Right View becomes Right Action.

正業（Right Action）的意思是不傷害、不偷竊，並以誠實與用心來行動。正命（Right Livelihood）的意思是以不傷害其他眾生的方式來謀生。當你面對選擇時，問自己：哪一個做法最善？哪一個能守住中道的平衡？最糟的賺錢方式是傷害別人的方式；而最容易的行善方式，就是從今天一個誠實、有幫助的小舉動開始。有智慧地比較各種行為——更好、最好、最平衡——就是正見（Right View）轉化為正業（Right Action）的方法。

練習 Practice

Homework

Each day this week, compare and rank your choices to check that you are walking the Middle Path. Choose the tier that fits you. 每天用比較級和最高級評價你的行為，選一個適合自己的等級。

Beginner 入門

Rank the day

Ages 5–8 · new learners
Write 2 sentences using the best and the worst to describe today. Add 1 sentence celebrating your best choice.
用 the best（最好）和 the worst（最糟）寫 2 個句子來描述今天。再加 1 個句子，慶祝你今天最好的選擇。
ExampleThe best part of my day was laughing. The worst feeling was anger. I am happy because I made the best choice.
Intermediate 中級

Compare & choose

Ages 9–12 · developing fluency
Write 2 sentences comparing a right action with an unwholesome one, then 1 sentence explaining why your best choice fits the Middle Path.
寫 2 個句子，比較一個正確的行為和一個不善的行為，再用 1 個句子說明為什麼你最好的選擇符合中道。
ExampleBeing honest is better than lying. Helping others is the most rewarding action I can take. I chose the best action because it avoided the extremes of laziness and stress.
Advanced 進階

Livelihood & reflection

Ages 13–16 · fluent speakers
Write 3–4 sentences using superlatives to discuss Right Livelihood and Right Action with complex structures, then 2 sentences reflecting on your hardest-but-best choice.
用最高級寫 3–4 個句子，搭配較複雜的句型，討論正命和正業，再用 2 個句子反思你那個最難卻最好的選擇。
ExampleBecoming a teacher is the most wholesome job because it helps people grow. When I chose to tell the truth, it was the hardest decision, but it brought the most peace to my heart.

小考 Mini Quiz

Check Yourself

選出最適合的答案，點選後會看到解析。

Question 1 of 10
Which word means “有益的／良善的”?
Correct: B. Wholesome means good and beneficial (有益的). Unwholesome is the opposite (無益的).
Question 2 of 10
Complete the comparison: “Kindness is ___ money.”
Correct: D. Long adjectives use more … than: more important than. We do not say “importanter.”
Question 3 of 10
Which is the correct superlative of good ?
Correct: A. Good is irregular: good → better → the best.
Question 4 of 10
What does Right Action (正業) mean?
Correct: C. Right Action means behaving without harming others — no stealing, honesty, and kindness in what we do.
Question 5 of 10
In the story, the lute string teaches us that the wisest way is…
Correct: D. The string must be tuned just right. This is the Middle Path — balance between two extremes.
Question 6 of 10
Right Livelihood (正命) is about…
Correct: A. Right Livelihood means making a living in a way that does not hurt others — a wholesome job.
Question 7 of 10
Choose the correct sentence to name the best choice.
Correct: B. Use the best on its own — never “the most best” or “more best.”
Question 8 of 10
Which word means “無益的／有害的”?
Correct: C. Unwholesome (無益的) describes actions that cause harm — the opposite of wholesome.
Question 9 of 10
Finish the sentence: “Helping others is the ___ action I can take.”
Correct: A. A superlative needs the most with long adjectives: the most rewarding.
Question 10 of 10
According to the Middle Path, the best choice is usually…
Correct: D. The Middle Path avoids extremes. The wisest action is the most balanced and wholesome one.
